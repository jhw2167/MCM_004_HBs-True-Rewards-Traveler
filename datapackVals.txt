====================================================
  HB'S TRAVELER REWARDS — LOOT TABLE DESIGN NOTES
====================================================

----------------------------------------------------
ENCHANTED ESSENCE
----------------------------------------------------
  Structures (plain essence, biome-appropriate enchant target):
    - Desert Temple          20%
    - Jungle Temple          20%
    - Ocean Monument         20%  (aquatic enchant target)
    - Abandoned Mineshaft    15%
    - Nether Bastion         15%
    - Nether Fortress        15%
    - End City               20%
    - Ancient City           20%
  Mob Drops (pre-enchanted with random target):
    - Witch                  10%  (already enchanted, random target)
  Notes: Core progression item. Broadly available across all major biomes
         and dimensions. Witch drops arrive pre-enchanted.

----------------------------------------------------

    - Desert Temple          5%

    - Ocean Monument         5%
    - Nether Bastion         5%
    - Nether Fortress        5%

SOULBOUND RITUAL TABLET
----------------------------------------------------
  Structures:
    - Ancient City           5%
  Mob Drops:
    - Enderman               0.1%
    - Ender Dragon (on kill) 1 guaranteed drop
  Notes: Rarest of the ritual tablets. The Ender Dragon guaranteed drop
         provides a meaningful end-game milestone reward.

====================================================
  GENERAL NOTES
====================================================
  - Facade Blocks are intended to be crafted, not found as loot.
  - Weathered Beacon is a structure find (Ocean Monument, Stronghold,
    or Woodland Mansion recommended), not a mob drop.
  - Iron Bloom and Gold Bloom excluded from loot tables for now.
  - All percentages are per-chest unless noted as mob drop rates.
